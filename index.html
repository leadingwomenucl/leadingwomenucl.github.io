ABOUT US

We wish to bring a

sustainable balance and diversity

to leadership.

We do this by empowering women to pursue highly competitive fields within their career, and enforcing personal development activities to be confident, ambitious and supportive towards helping others.


Our society is a forum for discussion and motivation, and an avenue for social good in our communities. We encourage men to join in on the discussion as well.



PRESIDENT'S WELCOME


Dearest Members,

I would like to warmly welcome you to our second term of 2015 at UCL. Whether you are one of our long-standing members at LWS or a curious first year student, there will be plenty of upcoming events for you to be a part of.

This term, as you can see, we launch our new LWS website. Do take a look around our pages, which will be updated weekly with an exciting line up of activities and information.

Take a look at our event calendar; our next event is Tech Week. Be sure to come along to our Tech Night!

The Leading Women Society, founded in 2013, is a winner of UCL’s ‘Best New Society of 2014’.Backed by the world's leading consulting and banking institutions, our goal is to organise bespoke, prestigious events, maintain strong ties with high profile employers, and stand by our members to ensure they accomplish their personal goals.

An exciting line-up with our partners awaits us this term; from drop in coffee sessions, inspirational talks from leaders, video portal launch connecting employers with students, Technology Week to End of term socials, there will be something for everyone.

The success of this ever-growing society would not be possible without the support from my dedicated LWS committee. I would like to thank everyone for the sheer amount of hard work and bright ideas they put forward within the last few months.

Our line of amazing sponsors has also been pivotal in raising our society commercially, and I am delighted to continue our future partnerships with so much support moving forward.

Finally, I will be personally happy to assist with collaboration ideas, new sponsorship and personal queries. I am reachable via email or in person at campus.


Kindest Regards,
Rasini Ranawake, LWS President
