President's Welcome

Dearest visitor,

Hello and welcome to UCLU Leading Women! I hope you enjoy exploring our website to learn more about what we do and why we do it. The society is for everyone and we welcome you with open arms.

After a successful year of CV & Mock-Interview workshops, Christmas Dinner and our inaugural Tech week , the team and I are more excited than ever to fill up your calendar with even bigger and better events! Kindly do have a look at our upcoming events and timeline to see what’s on. There is something for everyone, so do join us to meet new friends while saying hello to the old.

The Leading Women Society was founded in 2013 and won UCL’s ‘Best New Society of 2014’. With the support of our generous sponsors and partners, including some of the world’s leading consulting, banking and professional services institutions, we organise unique, prestigious events catered to add value to our members. We are also more than just a society but a community that grows together and supports each other in accomplishing our career aspirations as well as personal goals.

It has been a delight to see the society growing stronger than ever and meeting new members by the day. None of this would have been possible without the efforts of the past committee as well as the hard work of the current. They are truly a dedicated team in bringing you the best.

I would also like to thank our amazing sponsors who have been extremely encouraging and supportive of the society. They have been instrumental in the society’s success and I look forward to their partnerships in the year ahead.

Finally, I am happy to assist with collaboration ideas, new sponsorship and personal queries. Please do reach out to me via email or in person on campus. See you very soon!


Warmest regards,

Aileen

President of the UCLU Leading Women Society
