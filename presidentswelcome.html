President's Welcome

Dearest Members,

I would like to warmly welcome you to our second term of 2015 at UCL. Whether you are one of our long-standing members at LWS or a curious first year student, there will be plenty of upcoming events for you to be a part of.

This term, as you can see, we've launched our new LWS website. Do take a look around our pages, which will be updated weekly with an exciting line up of activities and information.

Take a look at our event calendar; our next event is Tech Week. Be sure to come along to our Tech Night! We are also taking part in EY's Cryptic Campus, in a bid to win more money to hold more amazing events for our members. Remember to play on our team!

The Leading Women Society, founded in 2013, is a winner of UCL’s ‘Best New Society of 2014’.Backed by some of the world's leading consulting and banking institutions, our strategy is to organise bespoke, prestigious events, maintain strong ties with valuable companies, and stand by our members to ensure they accomplish their personal goals.

The success of this ever-growing society would not be possible without the support from my dedicated LWS committee. I would like to thank everyone for the sheer amount of hard work and bright ideas they put forward within the last few months.

Our line of amazing sponsors has also been pivotal in raising our society commercially, and I am delighted to continue our future partnerships with so much support moving forward.

Finally, I will be personally happy to assist with collaboration ideas, new sponsorship and personal queries. I am reachable via email or in person at campus.


Kindest Regards,

Rasini Ranawake

President of the UCLU Leading Women Society
