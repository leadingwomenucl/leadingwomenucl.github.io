Main Speakers


We have chosen two exceptional British talents from the best startups in the UK to share their personal stories with us, and are delighted they will be joining us for the night!

Amelia went from an early career in marketing at Jimmy Choo to founding her own coding school, Steer, after finding a gap in the market for intensive yet thorough short courses. Having initially raised £100,000 seed investment from a group of angel investors, she is already managing to turn a profit, an impressive achievement for a start-up business, and is now determined to scale the company organically with no plans to raise further funding. Steer was voted as one of the best places to learn coding in 2014, and has some exciting plans for the future - but we'll let Amelia tell you about that herself.

Ben began engineering the very backbone of Swiftkey just after finishing his PhD in Natural Language Processing at Cambridge, using his background in Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing to create a keyboard that captures the way people use language. Swiftkey broke into the startup world when accelerator programmes were scarce, and has since become one of the top UK-based startups with offices worldwide. They were named Wired's No. 1 Hottest Startup in London, and is one of the most downloaded apps accross platforms. Swiftkey's mission is to build technology that makes it easy for everyone to create and communicate on mobile. They have also released an app, Swiftkey Note, to complement their keyboard, their Swiftkey SDK for developers, and specialised keyboards, such as Swiftkey Healthcare.

Both Amelia and Ben are inspiring individuals who have entered and succeeded in the industry whilst following completely different paths. They demonstrate that both having no background, or a thorough background, has no effect on your choice to join the digital revolution!
